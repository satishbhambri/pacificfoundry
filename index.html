The Program

Twelve months. No shortcuts.

We don't run a 3-month demo day sprint. Pacific Foundry is a 12-month operating partnership designed for founders building complex, technically deep companies.

01
Capital to Build
$250K investment per company, structured as a SAFE. No board seats in exchange. We are operators, not controllers. Follow-on reserved for top performers.
02
Technical Residency
Monthly in-person sprints in San Francisco and Atlanta. Deep technical reviews, architecture sessions with senior engineers, and peer founder cohort dinners.
03
Industry Access
Direct warm introductions to enterprise design partners, Series A investors, and federal research programs (SBIR/STTR). We open doors before you have to knock.
